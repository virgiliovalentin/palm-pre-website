Stay connected

The power of connection is in your hands. Pre allows you to talk to your friends through your favorite social media apps. Hello, Pre's innovative messaging app lets you connect your social media apps in one single feed and every update, tweet or post you make is automatically synced to your favorite platforms.

Work smart, not hard

Synergy automatically connects your business email inbox, calendar and contacts in one place and syncs any changes you make in your phone to your computer. Need to schedule a Teams meeting? Check. Want to send an email to a potential client? Also check. No need to worry about cables and taking big old agendas with you.

Inspired by you

Pre is sleek and compact, made to fit in the palm of your hands. The 3.1" screen allows you to browse the web and answer messages with no issues and the FastType physical keyboard makes sure you type with no errors.

webOS

Simple. Smart. Advanced.

Made to help you stay connected and be productive, webOS was built from scratch with one focus on mind: simplicity. Gestures, true multitasking and a big catalog of apps ensures you'll have the best and most advanced experience in a mobile device.
